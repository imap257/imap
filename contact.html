Prospective Students & Collaborators

Join the iMAP Community

We are always keen to hear from motivated students and researchers who share our passion for the science of meaning. Whether you are considering a PhD, a postdoctoral position, or a research collaboration, please reach out to the director most closely aligned with your interests — or to both.

When writing, we invite you to briefly describe your background, your research interests, and what draws you to iMAP's work. We look forward to hearing from you.
