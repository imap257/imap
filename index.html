Our Vision

What we believe and why it matters

Meaning Is Always More Than Words

Human communication is a rich, layered phenomenon. Words carry propositions, but meaning emerges in the dynamic interplay between what is said, how it is said, what is gestured, and who is listening. At iMAP, we are captivated by this complexity — and committed to understanding it rigorously.

We believe that truly understanding language requires studying it where it lives: in real interactions, across multiple modalities, and among the extraordinarily diverse communities that populate our world. By situating our research at two linguistically and culturally rich hubs — Singapore and California — we gain a window onto humanity's full communicative range.

Our work bridges formal semantics and pragmatics with experimental methods, corpus analysis, and fieldwork, asking how meaning is built, negotiated, and shared in the moment of interaction. We are driven by the conviction that answering these questions will deepen our understanding of mind, language, and what it means to connect with another human being.

Research Themes

Core questions that drive our inquiry

🗣️

Multimodal Communication

How do speech, gesture, prosody, and gaze combine to convey and negotiate meaning? We investigate the grammar of multimodal expression across typologically distinct languages.

🌏

Cross-linguistic Semantics

We probe the universals and particulars of meaning by examining semantic phenomena — quantification, focus, questions, and more — in languages spanning East Asia, Southeast Asia, and beyond.

💬

Pragmatics & Interaction

Language use is shaped by social context, common ground, and interactional goals. We study how speakers manage implicature, reference, and discourse structure in natural conversation.

🧪

Experimental Methods

Combining behavioral experiments, eye-tracking, corpus analysis, and annotation studies, we bring empirical rigor to questions that have long been explored only theoretically.

🌐

Language & Diversity

Singapore and California are among the most linguistically diverse places on earth. We leverage this diversity as a natural laboratory to test and refine our theoretical models.

🤝

Collaboration Across Borders

True to our name, we cultivate a transatlantic community of researchers, fostering partnerships with scholars across Asia, North America, and Europe.
